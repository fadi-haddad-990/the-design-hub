Upper Brockley Road
Restrained modern luxury with a classic soul - Designing this 4 storey home has been one of our proudest moments to date.
— Jordan and Russell

The challenge was to take this shell of a victorian townhouse and make it in to a modern space that reflected the different styles of the lovely couple who live here. The colour palette modulates, creating different atmospheres – It’s a fine balance of total opulence and honest raw materials like concrete, brass and corten steel. Statement lighting combines with vintage pieces and bespoke furniture to create a dramatic curated feel. We particularly loved designing custom joinery for several of the spaces giving our client a truly unique home. There is a flow throughout the property, with a touch of drama in each space.
