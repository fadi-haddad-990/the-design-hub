New York – A visual travelogue

The Fabric of a city, the buildings themselves, can be just as inspirational and have as big an impact on our design, as the design pieces themselves.

So we wanted to share that process with you here – a photographic diary of our inspirations on the streets of New York as we follow the design trail. It’s amazing how you forget to look up in your own city, but when you are somewhere new it can open your eyes in a whole new way. Hopefully it will give you an insight into our process and you will find it as inspiring as we did.
